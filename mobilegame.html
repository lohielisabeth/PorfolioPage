Project Overview

We developed a mini-game mobile app for iOS as part of a team project. The app includes multiple games such as Flabby Bird, Color Sort, Bubble Shooter, 2048, Minesweeper, Tic-Tac-Toe, and Connect Four.

The goal was to create an engaging, user-friendly mobile gaming experience while learning and applying React Native and Expo technologies.
